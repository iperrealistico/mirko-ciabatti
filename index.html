Mirko Ciabatti · Director of Photography (DOP)

Director of photography based in Italy, working across films, commercials, music videos and documentaries.

Mirko Ciabatti is a director of photography (DOP) based in Italy. He creates images for films, commercials, music videos and documentaries, building each frame from light, composition and camera movement.

As a cinematographer, Mirko collaborates with directors and production companies across Europe, bringing a grounded, natural approach to storytelling. He works in both studio and on location, in Italian and English speaking environments.
